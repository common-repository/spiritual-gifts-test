== Description ==
Spiritual Gifts and S.H.A.P.E. Test to help church attendees find their place of service in the local church and other service organizations.

For dynamic control over your **Spiritual Gifts or SHAPE sections**, along with volunteer position postings and matching qualifications to tests, visit [ServeWire VES](https://www.ministryvitals.com).  Our Volunteer Engagement System includes deep integration with Planning Center, Church Community Builder, and other popular ChMS platforms.

Visit [our website](https://www.ministryvitals.com/free-spiritual-gifts-test/) for complete instructions.

== Installation ==
1. Upload "spiritualgiftstest.zip" to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the \"Plugins\" menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the email settings and insert the shortcode on any page.
4. Visit [this page](https://www.ministryvitals.com/free-spiritual-gifts-test/) for complete instructions.
